About Me

Editor. Strategist.
Creative.

ADD YOUR PHOTO HERE

I'm Sujay — a video editor and content strategist with over 3 years of hands-on experience creating content that actually stops the scroll. I spent years inside an agency editing social media reels for businesses and coaches, learning exactly what makes content perform and what makes it disappear.

But agency life has its limits. I wanted creative freedom — and I wanted to work directly with the brands I believed in. So I went independent. Now I bring the same production quality and strategic thinking I built at the agency, without the bureaucracy that slows everything down.

My edge is simple: I don't just edit videos, I think about why each cut, each caption, and each hook exists. Every piece of content I create is built around a creative idea first — because in a world full of noise, only original thinking cuts through.
